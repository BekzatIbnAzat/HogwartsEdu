House Gryffindor: Bravery

“Gryffindor, where dwell the brave at heart.” A dash of daring resides within
individuals selected for this house, so it comes as no surprise that our courageous
protagonist Mr. Potter and his closest friends wind up as Gryffindors.

Their adventurous spirits take Harry, Ron, and Hermione to many wonderful places
and the same can be true for marketing muggles like ourselves.
